This is a completely C# console based recreation of Friday Night Funkin. It keeps score, has a combo system, and a pretty accurate but forgiving input window. If set up correctly you can make this program into a command prompt command.

Heretic mod for Risk of Rain

Over the summer I decided to port the Heretic from Risk of Rain 2 into Risk of Rain 1. This isn't really a port though, more of a recreation. I took the liberty to make some balance changes to it and I think it turned out pretty good. She makes for an cool character and some of her synergies with old items are interesting.
